Relaks i chill

The area of water around 30'S to 30'N is known to be one of the best places for sailing. If you choose the right time (and avoid the hurricane season) you can enjoy smooth cruising across the great blue. Perfect for people who prefer to relax and chillout, but at the same time do not mind the adventure of long sailing trips.

The same goes for our bracelet. It shows that one want to enjoy smooth sailing, however they do not mind long voyages and sailors day-to-day routine. Product is made of typical maritime colors which shows the attachment to the sailing tradition and blue, calm waves.
